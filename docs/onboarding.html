Your evidence auditor is ready

DarkLens detects and documents evidence of potentially manipulative interface patterns. It does not determine legal liability.

DarkLens never injects banners or floating widgets into the pages you visit — its signal lives quietly in the toolbar icon badge. Chrome hides new extensions behind the puzzle‑piece menu by default, so take ten seconds to pin it:

1
Click the puzzle‑piece icon in your Chrome toolbar.
2
Find DarkLens in the list and click the pin icon beside it.
3
Browse normally. The icon badge shows a count when DarkLens finds something — click it to open the side panel with full evidence.

V0.2 uses rule‑based detection across 3 pattern classes. Research / portfolio prototype — not a compliance or legal tool. End‑to‑end benchmark results are pending until the evaluation harness is running. See SYSTEM‑CARD.md for known limitations.
